# ki

The preposition **ki** marks the **destination** or **recipient**, roughly equivalent to "towards", or "until (a time)".

$mi $le $pi $konco **ki** $osa $tiju $etu. - "I'm at work until 18:00"

$mi $le $lo **ki** $insu $mi. "I am going to my house"

$mi $le $tene $matin **ki** $tu $in $kuwosi. \- "I gave a fruit to you"
